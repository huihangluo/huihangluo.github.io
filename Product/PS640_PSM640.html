Motor Protection Relay PSM640

Description

The PSM640 IED (intelligent electronic device) is designed for the protection, control, measurement, and supervision of utility distribution substations and industrial power systems motors.

Key Features

  • Main and backup protection for motor applications
  • Motor differential, current, voltage, and frequency protection
  • Out-of-step, thermal overload, motor load jam, and F-C block protection
  • External mechanical protection
